# SLEIPNIR — Go-to-Market Strategy

## Phased GTM

### Phase 1 (M1-M3): MVP Launch — Casablanca & Rabat
- Launch MVP in Casablanca and Rabat
- Organic growth via social media
- Target: **1,000 MAU**

### Phase 2 (M4-M6): National Expansion
- Expand to all major cities (Marrakech, Fes, Tangier, Agadir, etc.)
- Add dealer partnerships
- Target: **10,000 MAU**

### Phase 3 (M7-M12): B2B Monetization
- Launch B2B SaaS platform for dealers
- Introduce premium listings
- Target: **50,000 MAU**
